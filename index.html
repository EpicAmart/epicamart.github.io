Jane Street Market Classification

We explored the power of quantitative modeling to generate profitable predictions in financial markets. After estimating each asset’s drift over a specific time horizon using Geometric Brownian Motion, a Mixture Density Network made probabilistic trading decisions based on the conditional distribution of 130 features.

Technologies: PyTorch, GPU Kernels
